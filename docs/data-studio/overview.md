# Data Studio

![Data Studio](./images/data-studio.png)

Use the tools in Data Studio to reshape your data to match how people think about your business, and to track your data as it flows from table to report so everyone can trust the numbers.

- **Create an easy-to-understand semantic layer** to keep things accurate and consistent across dashboards, agentic BI, and automated workflows.
- **Speed up queries**. Transform tables to match usage patterns, and precompute common views of your data.
- **Identify and fix data problems**. By keeping all of your computation in one place, you can track how data flows through your Metabase and identify broken logic and schema drifts before they impact reports.

To enter Data Studio, click the grid icon in the top right, and select **Data Studio**.
